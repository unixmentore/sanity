User Registration and Logi

Users would typically download the app or use website and create an account by providing necessary information, such as their name, email address, a secure password

Assessment and Profile Creation

After registration, the app may prompt users to complete an initial assessment or questionnaire to gather relevant information about their mental health concerns, goals, and preferences. This data is used to create a user profile and personalize the app experience

Matching and Therapist Selection

Based on the user's profile and preferences, the app's algorithm may match them with suitable therapists or provide a list of available therapists for them to choose from. Users can review therapist profiles, which may include their qualifications, specialties, and approach to therapy, to make an informed selection.

The app will offer secure and private communication channels for users to interact with their assigned therapist. This could include options such as text-based messaging, audio calls, or video sessions. The user and therapist can schedule sessions based on their mutual availability.
